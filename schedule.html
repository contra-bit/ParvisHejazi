Repertoire

Being a pianist and composer, Parvis highlights the importance of wide-range repertoire. Thus he is keen on bringing barely performed music to performance, such as Takashi Yoshimatsu's "Random Bird Variations" for piano duo or Dietrich Buxtehude's Choral Variations on "Wie schön leuchtet der Morgenstern" BuxWV223 in addition to standard repertoire. Having intensively dealt with style and performance practice of the Viennese School in general, but with fortepiano in particular, Parvis puts emphasize on performing music by composers of the First and Second Viennese School.
